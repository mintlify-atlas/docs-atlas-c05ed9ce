## What Makes SmartTube Different?

<Note>
SmartTube isn't just another YouTube client - it's specifically designed and optimized for the TV viewing experience, with features that the official YouTube app doesn't offer.
</Note>

### Quality Without Compromise

- **True Ad-Free**: Not just ad-blocking, but literally unable to display ads
- **Community-Powered**: SponsorBlock integration backed by millions of user submissions
- **Maximum Quality**: Support for up to 8K resolution and 60fps playback
- **Modern Codecs**: AV1 and VP9 support for better compression and quality

### Privacy & Independence

- No Google Services dependency
- Works without tracking your every move
- Open source and transparent
- Community-driven development

## Known Limitations

<Warning>
Before using SmartTube, be aware of these limitations:
</Warning>

- **Not supported on phones and tablets** - designed exclusively for TV interfaces
- **Comment functionality is unstable** - reading comments may not work reliably
- **Voice search and casting** - may have inferior performance compared to official apps, depending on your device
- **Cannot submit SponsorBlock segments** - you can only use existing community submissions
