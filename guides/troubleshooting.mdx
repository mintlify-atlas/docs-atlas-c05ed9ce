## Common Playback Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='"Unknown codec" Error'>
    This error typically appears briefly when starting a video.
    
    **Solutions:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Wait 5 Seconds">
        The video should start playing automatically after a brief moment
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Press Play">
        If waiting doesn't work, manually press the play button
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    **Additional causes:**
    
    - **USB audio device attached**: Some users report this error only occurs when USB audio devices are connected. Try disconnecting USB audio devices.
    - **Full disk storage**: Check if your device storage is full. Free up space by clearing cache or uninstalling unused apps.
    
    <Tip>
    This is usually a temporary initialization issue and resolves itself within seconds.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title='"Video profile is not supported" Error'>
    This means your device doesn't support the selected video codec (usually AV01).
    
    **Solution:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Format Settings">
        Press the **HQ** button in the bottom-left corner of the player
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Select Video Formats">
        Choose **Video formats** from the menu
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Change Codec">
        Select anything **other than AV01** - choose **VP9** or **AVC** instead
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Warning>
    AV01 is NOT supported on most devices, especially those manufactured before 2020. VP9 is the best choice for most Android TV devices.
    </Warning>
    
    See the [Video Codecs guide](/configuration/video-codecs) for more information on choosing the right codec.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title='"Video unavailable" for Unlisted Videos'>
    Currently a known bug when watching unlisted videos or your own videos.
    
    **Status:** This is a confirmed bug that's being worked on.
    
    **Workaround:** Use the official YouTube app for unlisted content, or use [casting](/features/casting) from your phone.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Sign in to confirm you're not a bot Error">
    This occurs when your IP address range is blocked by YouTube for unauthenticated viewing.
    
    **Solutions:**
    
    1. **Sign in to your account**: This usually resolves the issue immediately
    2. **Change your network**: Try a different internet connection
    3. **Wait it out**: Temporary blocks may be lifted after some time
    4. **Use VPN**: Change your IP address (may be against YouTube's ToS)
    
    <Info>
    This is a YouTube-side restriction, not a SmartTube issue. Your IP range might be temporarily or permanently flagged.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Buffering Issues

### Videos Buffer Excessively

Buffering issues are not specific to SmartTube - other unofficial YouTube apps report the same problems. This appears to be server-side discrimination by YouTube.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Solution 1: Use Encrypted DNS (Recommended)">
    Switching to encrypted DNS like NextDNS often resolves buffering issues.
    
    **Automatic Setup:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install Intra">
        Download [Intra from F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/app.intra/)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Configure Intra">
        Open Intra and enable DNS encryption
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Auto-start Setup">
        Install ["AutoStart - No root"](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.autostart) to make Intra launch automatically after TV restarts
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    **Manual Setup:**
    
    Follow [this Reddit guide](https://www.reddit.com/r/MiBox/s/7esEVGtAAa) for manual DNS configuration.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Solution 2: Adjust Video Settings">
    Try these in-app adjustments:
    
    - **Reduce resolution**: Lower video quality temporarily
    - **Change codec to AVC**: Press HQ button > Video formats > AVC
    - **Increase buffer size**: Go to _Settings > Video player > Buffer size_ and select **High**
    - **Restart video**: Hit back button and play the video again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Solution 3: Network Troubleshooting">
    Check your network connection:
    
    - Test your internet speed at [fast.com](https://fast.com)
    - Ensure you have at least 5 Mbps for HD content
    - Try connecting via Ethernet instead of WiFi
    - Restart your router
    - Move closer to your WiFi router if using wireless
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
**Buffer setting recommendation:** Use **High** buffer for best performance. Higher buffer preloads more video, smoothing out network issues. RAM usage increase is minimal.
</Tip>

## Installation Problems

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Installation Fails">
    **Common causes:**
    
    1. **Disk space is full**
       - Free up at least 200 MB of storage
       - Clear cache from other apps
       - Uninstall unused apps
    
    2. **APK download corrupted**
       - Download the APK again
       - Ensure stable internet connection during download
       - Verify file size is reasonable (usually 60-80 MB)
    
    3. **Unknown sources blocked**
       - Enable "Unknown sources" for your file manager/downloader
       - See [Installation guide](/installation) for device-specific steps
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="App Installed but Crashes on Opening">
    This happens when SmartTube is installed to external storage.
    
    **Solution:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Uninstall SmartTube">
        Remove the current installation
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Check Storage Settings">
        Go to device settings and ensure apps install to **internal memory** by default
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Reinstall">
        Install SmartTube again - it should now go to internal storage
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Warning>
    SmartTube MUST be installed to internal memory, not SD card or external storage, or it will crash.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot Find Downloaded APK">
    If you downloaded the APK but can't find it:
    
    - Use a file manager app like [FX File Explorer](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=nextapp.fx)
    - Check the **Downloads** folder
    - Android's built-in file manager often doesn't work properly
    - Avoid FileCommander (it's ad-infested)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## App Crashes and Freezes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Device Freezes When Watching YouTube">
    This is typically a firmware or Android OS issue, not SmartTube-specific.
    
    **Potential causes:**
    - Buggy firmware
    - Custom ROM issues
    - Hardware problems
    
    **Troubleshooting steps:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Reboot Device">
        Turn off your Android TV completely and turn it back on
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Clear Cache">
        Go to _Settings > Apps > SmartTube > Clear cache_ (not Clear data)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Reinstall App">
        Uninstall and reinstall SmartTube
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Factory Reset (Last Resort)">
        If all else fails, factory reset your device (backup data first!)
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Warning>
    This issue is nearly impossible for the developer to debug since it's device/firmware-specific. If using a custom ROM, the ROM might be buggy.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="App Crashes Immediately">
    See "App Installed but Crashes on Opening" above - usually caused by installation to external storage.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Playback Performance Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AV01 Videos Don't Play">
    Most TVs and TV boxes do **not** have hardware support for AV01 codec (too new).
    
    **Solution:** Switch to VP9 or AVC codec (see "Video profile is not supported" section above)
    
    <Info>
    AV01 hardware support started appearing in devices from 2020 onwards. Most devices still don't support it.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="VP9 is Slow/Laggy">
    If VP9 videos play poorly:
    
    - Your device may lack VP9 hardware support
    - High resolutions (4K) require official hardware support
    - Cheap TV boxes without official 4K support will struggle
    
    **Solution:** Switch to AVC codec, which is supported on all devices
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Frame Drops at Different Playback Speeds">
    Known issue when playing videos at speeds other than 1x.
    
    **Status:** Currently cannot be fixed. This is a limitation of the video playback engine.
    
    **Workaround:** Use normal playback speed (1x) for smooth playback.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Buffer Clears When Seeking Backwards">
    When you jump back in a video, SmartTube must rebuffer.
    
    **Status:** This is current behavior and may be improved in future updates.
    
    **Workaround:** Minimize seeking backwards, or use higher buffer setting to reduce rebuffering time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Search Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No Search Results (Android 11)">
    Recent updates to "Google app for Android TV" can cause voice search failures.
    
    **Solution:**
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open App Settings">
        Go to _Settings > Apps > Google app for Android TV_
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Uninstall Updates">
        Select **Uninstall updates** to revert to the original version
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Test Search">
        Try voice search again - it should now work
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
