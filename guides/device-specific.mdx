## Chromecast with Google TV

Chromecast with Google TV blocks third-party app installation by default. You need to enable developer options first.

### Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Developer Options">
    On your Chromecast:
    1. Open the side menu
    2. Go to _Settings > System > About_
    3. Scroll down to **Android TV OS build**
    4. Click it repeatedly until you see a toast message about becoming a developer
    5. Keep clicking until developer mode is activated
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Unknown Sources">
    1. Go back to main _Settings_ page
    2. Select _Apps > Security & Restrictions > Unknown sources_
    3. Turn on the toggle for your file browser (e.g., **Downloader by AFTVnews**)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install SmartTube">
    Now follow the standard [Installation guide](/installation) to download and install SmartTube
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Quick install:** Use Downloader by AFTVnews and enter `kutt.it/stn_beta` or `kutt.it/stn_stable`
</Tip>

### Visual Guide

For detailed screenshots of the Chromecast installation process, see [this Android Police guide](https://www.androidpolice.com/2021/02/07/how-to-sideload-any-apk-on-chromecast-with-android-tv/#install-the-apk).

### Voice Search Setup

To enable voice search on Chromecast with Google TV:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Uninstall Official YouTube App">
    You must remove the official YouTube app (no root required)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install Bridge App">
    Download and install the ATV Bridge SmartTube app: `kutt.it/stn_bridge_atv`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Voice Search">
    Use voice search on your remote - it should now open SmartTube
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The bridge app intercepts voice search requests and redirects them to SmartTube. It won't appear in your launcher - it works automatically in the background.
</Info>

## Amazon Fire TV

Fire TV devices work well with SmartTube and are among the easiest to set up.

### Installation

Fire TV allows sideloading by default. Simply:

1. Install [Downloader by AFTVnews](https://www.aftvnews.com/downloader/) from the Amazon AppStore
2. Open Downloader and enter: `kutt.it/stn_beta` or `kutt.it/stn_stable`
3. Follow the installation prompts

### Voice Search Setup

Fire TV has special bridge app support:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Uninstall Official YouTube App">
    Remove the YouTube app (no root required on Fire TV)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install Amazon Bridge">
    Download and install: `kutt.it/stn_bridge_amazon`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Alexa Voice Search">
    Now when you use Alexa to search YouTube, it opens in SmartTube
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
On Fire TV, you may need to explicitly say "YouTube" when searching (e.g., "YouTube cute cats" instead of just "cute cats").
</Tip>

### Fire TV Compatibility

SmartTube works on **all generations** of Fire TV Stick, including:
- Fire TV Stick Lite
- Fire TV Stick (2nd, 3rd, 4th Gen)
- Fire TV Stick 4K
- Fire TV Cube

## NVIDIA Shield

NVIDIA Shield is a powerful Android TV device that runs SmartTube excellently, with one notable exception.

### HDR Limitation

<Warning>
**Important:** NVIDIA Shield supports HDR in general, but **NOT the specific HDR format used by YouTube**.
</Warning>

This is a hardware limitation of the Shield, not a SmartTube issue. HDR YouTube videos will play but without HDR enhancement.

### What Works on Shield

✅ All video codecs (AV01, VP9, AVC) with hardware acceleration  
✅ 4K/8K resolution support  
✅ 60fps playback  
✅ All SmartTube features  
✅ Excellent performance  

❌ YouTube's HDR format  

<Info>
For more details about HDR compatibility, see [HDR documentation](/configuration/hdr).
</Info>

### Voice Search Setup (Requires Root)

Unlike Fire TV and Chromecast, NVIDIA Shield requires root access for voice search integration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Root Your Shield">
    Search for a rooting guide specific to your Shield model
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Uninstall Official YouTube">
    Use ADB command: `adb shell pm uninstall com.google.android.youtube.tv`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install Bridge App">
    Download and install: `kutt.it/stn_bridge_atv`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Rooting voids your warranty and can brick your device if done incorrectly. Proceed at your own risk.
</Warning>

## Xiaomi Devices with Chinese Firmware

Xiaomi devices with Chinese firmware have special restrictions that can block SmartTube beta installation.

### The Issue

- **Chinese firmware**: Blocks SmartTube **beta** version installation
- **International firmware**: No issues, works normally
- **Stable version**: Usually works even on Chinese firmware

### Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Solution 1: Use Stable Version (Recommended)">
    Instead of beta, use the **stable version** of SmartTube:
    
    1. Download from: `kutt.it/stn_stable`
    2. Install normally
    3. Stable version bypasses Chinese firmware restrictions
    
    <Tip>
    The stable version is still excellent and receives all major features, just slightly less frequently than beta.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Solution 2: Flash International Firmware">
    Switch to the international firmware version for your Xiaomi device.
    
    **Pros:**
    - Removes restrictions
    - Allows beta version
    - Often improves overall device experience
    
    **Cons:**
    - Requires technical knowledge
    - Risk of bricking if done incorrectly
    - May void warranty
    
    Search for "Xiaomi [your model] international firmware" for specific guides.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Solution 3: Factory Reset Method (2020 and Earlier Devices)">
    For devices from 2020 or before:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Factory Reset">
        Perform a complete factory reset on your Xiaomi device
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Install SmartTube Beta First">
        Before doing ANY system updates, install SmartTube beta
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Update System">
        Now you can safely update your system - SmartTube should continue working
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Warning>
    This method only works for devices manufactured in 2020 or earlier. Newer devices require Solution 1 or 2.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Android 11 Voice Search Issues

Devices running Android 11 may experience voice search problems.

### Symptom

Voice search doesn't return results, or searches fail to execute properly.

### Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the Problem">
    Recent updates to "Google app for Android TV" cause this bug
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Uninstall Updates">
    1. Go to _Settings > Apps > Google app for Android TV_
    2. Select **Uninstall updates**
    3. Confirm to revert to the factory version
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Voice Search">
    Try voice search again - it should now work correctly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Prevent Auto-Update (Optional)">
    Disable auto-updates for the Google app to prevent the issue from returning
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
This is a Google app issue, not specific to SmartTube. The bug affects multiple apps using voice search on Android TV 11.
</Info>

## Unsupported Devices

SmartTube **ONLY** works on Android-based devices. It is **NOT** compatible with:

❌ Samsung Tizen TVs  
❌ LG webOS TVs  
❌ Roku devices  
❌ iOS / Apple TV  
❌ Smartphones and tablets (not optimized, no support offered)  

<Warning>
The app cannot easily be ported to non-Android platforms. If you have an unsupported TV, consider using an Android TV stick or box instead.
</Warning>

### Alternative for Unsupported Devices

If you have a non-Android TV:

1. **Buy an Android TV stick or box** (recommended options):
   - Chromecast with Google TV (~$50)
   - Amazon Fire TV Stick (~$30-50)
   - Xiaomi Mi Box S (~$60)
   - Generic Android TV boxes (~$30-100)

2. Connect it to your TV's HDMI port
3. Install SmartTube on the Android device
