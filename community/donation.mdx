## About the Project

From the developer:

> "SmartTube is developed single-handedly; there is no larger team or company behind this. This is an open source, hobby project."

Your support enables continued development of features like:

- Ad-free viewing experience
- SponsorBlock integration
- 8K and HDR support
- 60fps playback
- Adjustable playback speed
- Regular bug fixes and improvements

<Note>
  Several community members have also contributed translations and code improvements. View contributors on [GitHub](https://github.com/yuliskov/SmartTube/graphs/contributors).
</Note>
