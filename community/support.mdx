## How to Report Bugs

When reporting issues, please provide:

- **Device model** and Android version
- **SmartTube version** (found in Settings > About)
- **Build flavor** (beta, stable, or fdroid)
- **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- **Expected behavior** vs actual behavior
- **Screenshots or screen recordings** if applicable

<Warning>
  If reporting a crash, include any error messages or logs if possible.
</Warning>

## Community Guidelines

Our community is welcoming and helpful. Please:

- **Be respectful** and patient with others
- **Use English** in the international groups ([@SmartTubeEN](http://t.me/SmartTubeEN))
- **Search before asking** - check FAQ and recent messages
- **Stay on topic** - keep discussions related to SmartTube
- **No piracy discussions** - focus on the app's features and functionality

<Info>
  Don't worry if your English isn't perfect! We have a friendly international community that's happy to help.
</Info>
