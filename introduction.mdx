# Welcome to SmartTube

SmartTube is a free and open-source advanced media player for Android TVs and TV boxes. It allows you to play content from various public sources with a superior viewing experience, completely ad-free.

## Why SmartTube?

SmartTube gives you complete control over your viewing experience on Android TV devices. Unlike official apps, SmartTube prioritizes your preferences and puts you in control.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Advanced Playback">
    - 8K resolution support
    - 60fps playback
    - HDR compatibility
    - Adjustable playback speed
    - Picture-in-Picture mode
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ad-Free Experience">
    - No video ads or banners
    - SponsorBlock integration
    - Completely ad-free by design
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customization">
    - Customizable buttons and UI
    - Multiple video codec support (AV1, VP9, AVC)
    - Configurable video quality presets
    - Theme options
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compatibility">
    - Android 4.3 (KitKat) and above
    - Works on Android TVs, Fire TV, NVIDIA Shield
    - No Google Services required
    - Casting support from mobile devices
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Supported Devices

SmartTube works on all Android TV devices and TV boxes, including:

- **Android TVs & Google TVs** (Sony, Philips, etc.)
- **Chromecast with Google TV**
- **Amazon Fire TV** (all generations)
- **NVIDIA Shield**
- **Xiaomi Mi Box**
- **Generic Android TV boxes**

<Warning>
SmartTube does **not** work on:
- Samsung Tizen TVs
- LG webOS TVs
- Roku devices
- iOS devices
- Smartphones and tablets (not optimized for touch input)
</Warning>
